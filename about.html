Our Family & Our Dogs

We are a small, family-owned breeder nestled in the countryside. Our dogs aren't just pets; they are family. Every puppy is born and raised in our living room, ensuring they receive constant love, socialization, and handling from day one. We believe in ethical breeding, focusing on temperament, health, and longevity.
