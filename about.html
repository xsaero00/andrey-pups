Our Family & Our Dogs

Welcome to Planida Paws! Located in beautiful Fair Oaks, California, we are the Planida family—a small, dedicated breeder with a passion for Golden Retrievers. Our dogs aren't just pets; they are family.

Every puppy is born and raised right in our living room, ensuring they receive constant love, socialization, and handling from day one. We believe in ethical breeding, focusing heavily on temperament, health, and longevity.

We thank God 👑 for the blessing of raising these wonderful creatures and for the joy of connecting them with loving forever families.
